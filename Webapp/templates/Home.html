Why our Apartment ?

Ad Blocker

Our apartment offers a unique perk with built-in ad blockers on our routers, ensuring a smoother, more secure browsing experience. This feature helps eliminate intrusive ads across all devices connected to the network, reducing distractions and enhancing your online privacy. Whether you're streaming, working from home, or just browsing the web, you’ll enjoy faster load times and safer internet use. It's just one of the thoughtful touches that make our home stand out

Energy tracking

Our apartment features an energy tracking meter, allowing you to monitor your electricity usage in real time. This smart feature helps you stay informed about your energy consumption, making it easier to manage your utility costs and live more sustainably. By providing insights into your daily usage, it empowers you to make eco-friendly choices while enjoying a modern, efficient home.

Shared Entertainment Accounts

Our apartment goes the extra mile by providing shared entertainment accounts for popular streaming platforms, making movie nights and binge-watching even better. Enjoy access to your favorite shows, movies, and music without the extra subscription costs. It’s a hassle-free way to relax, connect with friends, and make the most of your downtime. This added convenience makes our home a perfect place for comfort, fun, and shared experiences.
